The Dashboard contains the data trend of the mean, maximum and minimum temperature in Celcius of India.

You can select multiple years from the filter tab by holding ctrl and selecting the years. You can clear the comparison by clicking the eraser icon on the right of the filter. You can also see the particular year series by using the zoom slider.
